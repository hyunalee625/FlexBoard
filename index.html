Welcome to Flex Board!

What is Flex Board?

Flex Board is a visual task management board that maximizes work efficiency. If you are feeling overwhelmed or looking to be more organized, Flex Board can help you stay on top of your work.

How do I use it?

Under each stage (To Do, Doing, and Done), click on the + button to create a card inputting your task. Once the card is created, you can drag and drop the card to a different stage!

Oh no, I made a typo, can I edit?

Of course, click on the task directly and you can edit as you wish. It's as simple as that! If you no longer need the task, press the del button and voila! it's gone.
